The Solution

Introducing Tidings

Right message. Right person. Right time.

Tidings is a niche-specific, industry-compatible outreach infrastructure — an email-based, one-on-one direct channel built for secure, high-stakes communications.

It gives you control over who receives your message, allows you to segment your audience by partner, customer, or stakeholder, and delivers insights on who engaged and when.

At Defense Outreach, we built Tidings so your partners and customers never miss the message that matters.

Tidings refers to the cost of an undelivered message. In Shakespeare's Romeo and Juliet, Friar Lawrence sent a letter to Romeo about Juliet — it never arrived. That gap in communication took Romeo's life, and then Juliet's. The message must not remain undelivered.
